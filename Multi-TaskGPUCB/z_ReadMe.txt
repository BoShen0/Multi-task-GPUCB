this is the folder for the MT-GPUCB paper
1. demos: all numerical studies in the paper:
2. FastCV: the first real-world case study, i.e., fast cross validation
3. Flow3D experiemnts: some basic setup for the software
4. Flow3D simulations: the second real-world case study, i.e., material properties determination
5. TanEQLpaper: our modification based on the paper----Bayesian Optimization of Expected Quadratic Loss 
   for Multiresponse Computer Experiments with Internal Noise
6. utils: libraries for our proposed method
7. remaining codes are my build-in functions for our proposed method
